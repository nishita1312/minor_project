SPIRITUAL PLACES

Ujjain

Ujjain is renowned for the Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga, one of the 12 sacred shrines of Lord Shiva. It's a major Hindu pilgrimage site, hosting the grand Kumbh Mela, a gathering of millions of devotees that unfolds once every 12 years.

Omkareshwar

Situated on an island in the Narmada River, Omkareshwar is home to the Omkareshwar Jyotirlinga, a significant pilgrimage spot. Visitors can explore ancient temples and relish the tranquil island ambiance.

Amarkantak

Known as the source of the Narmada River, Amarkantak holds profound religious significance. The Maikal Hills surrounding the area are dotted with temples, and the natural beauty adds to the spiritual experience.

Maheshwar

Maheshwar is celebrated for the Maheshwar Temple and the picturesque ghats along the Narmada River. This tranquil town offers spiritual solace and a serene atmosphere.

Chitrakoot

Believed to be a place where Lord Rama spent a significant part of his exile, Chitrakoot is dotted with temples and holds great importance in Hinduism as a sacred site.

Orchha

Orchha, with its temples and palaces, is known for the revered Ram Raja Temple, where Lord Ram is worshiped as a king. The historical and spiritual ambiance of Orchha makes it a captivating destination.

Jabalpur

Jabalpur is home to the Chausath Yogini Temple, a unique circular shrine dedicated to the goddess Durga. The temple's distinctive architecture adds to its spiritual significance.
