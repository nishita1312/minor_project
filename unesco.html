UNESCO SITES

Sanchi Stupa

Located in Sanchi, this UNESCO World Heritage Site consists of numerous stupas, monasteries, temples, and pillars that date back to the 3rd century BC. The site holds immense importance in the history of Buddhism and showcases intricate carvings and architectural excellence.

Khajuraho Group of Monuments

This collection of intricately carved temples, built between the 9th and 11th centuries, is renowned for its remarkable erotic art, which is just one aspect of the broader narrative depicted in the sculptures. The temples are celebrated for their architectural brilliance and cultural significance..

Bhimbetka Rock Shelters

These rock shelters in the Vindhya Range contain prehistoric cave paintings that date back to approximately 30,000 years ago. They depict scenes from the daily life and rituals of ancient inhabitants, making Bhimbetka a unique and invaluable repository of early human history.
