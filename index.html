Reasons TO Explore MP

Three UNESCO World Heritage Sites

Khajuraho Group of Monuments, adorned with intricate erotic carvings;

the Bhimbetka Rock Shelters, featuring prehistoric cave paintings;

and the Sanchi Stupa , an iconic symbol of India's spiritual heritage

11 National parks and 24 Wild Life Sanctuaries

Explore the wilderness, experience thrilling safaris ,
with the highest number of Tigers in India , offers a unique opportunity to witness these majestic creatures in their natural habitat.

Rich Natural Beauty

Madhya Pradesh's rich natural beauty, from its dense forests to
serene lakes , paints a breathtaking backdrop for travelers to explore.

Two Jyotirlingas out of 12

Adorned with spiritual treasures, houses two of the sacred 12 Jyotirlingas -
Omkareshwar and Mahakaleshwar ,
inviting pilgrims to seek divine blessings.
